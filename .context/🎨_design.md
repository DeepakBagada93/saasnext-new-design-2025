# 🎨 Design System

## Theme & Colors
- **Mode:** Forced Dark Mode (via `dark` class on `html`)
- **Primary Color:** `#29ABE2` (Trust/Reliability)
- **Secondary/Background:** `#E5F5F9` (Clean/Professional)
- **Accent Color:** `#F26A2E` (CTAs/Highlights)

## Typography
- **Headlines:** `Space Grotesk` (Modern/Tech)
- **Body Text:** `PT Sans` (Readability)

## Animation Rules
- Utilize **GSAP** and **ScrollTrigger** for high-end, performant scroll animations.
- Use **Framer Motion** for micro-interactions and component-level transitions.
- Maintain a professional, smooth feel without overwhelming the user.
